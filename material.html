Material

Here you can find the materials needed to play VibeCodeNames, including the cards and any additional resources.

Note: the Cards PDF file contains double sided cards. Depending on your printer, you might have to adjust the offset of the back side to ensure the cards line up cleanly. You can use the calibration file to calculate the offset necessary, and then manually adjust them in the generation script located at Asartea-homework/ethics-codenames. Alternatively, print the cards single-sided.

Downloads

  • Cards: A downloadable PDF of all the cards used in the game.
  • Leaflet: A downloadable PDF of the reference leaflet.
  • Calibration Sheet: A downloadable PDF to help calibrate your printer for double-sided card printing.
