Welcome to VibeCodeNames!

VibeCodeNames is a variant of Codenames, which includes extra words pertaining to ethics and AI. Each ethics card includes a description of the topic and a question meant to get the players thinking about the topic.

The game consists of 252 cards, 55 of which are ethics related and the other 197 are normal words. The ethics related words can be recognised by the reference number in the top left and bottom right, which points to the matching description and question in the reference leaflet.

The major rule difference between VibeCodeNames and regular Codenames is that when an ethics card is correctly guessed, the team that guessed it is asked to give a brief answer to the question on the card.

VibeCodeNames was created as the final project for the Ethical Aspects in Natural Language Processing course at the Rijksuniversiteit Groningen by Jasper Kleine, Joris van Bruggen, and Tieme Boerema
